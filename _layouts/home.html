Because email is not about reading.

We believe that email is not about reading or replying.
Email is meant to be cleaned up to zero as fast as possible.

Archive or delete everything you can.
Delegate what someone can do for you.
Postpone everything that you cannot handle right now.
Create Todo tasks from what you need to take care of.
Read and reply to what's left and is important.

So we're creating new mobile email client that is focused exactly on these tasks.

It will make inbox cleanup a breeze, allow to postpone emails, integrate with your todo list and make email delegation easier. For just 3.99$.
